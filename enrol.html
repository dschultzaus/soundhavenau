Apply

Ready to get started? Apply for a place.

Complete this form to apply for a place in the next Sound Haven intake. We review all applications and contact you within 48 hours to arrange a free consultation call before confirming your place.

How Intakes Work

Sound Haven runs structured intake rounds — programs begin on fixed dates throughout the year. When you apply, we'll confirm whether the upcoming intake has places available, or offer you a spot in the next one.

Once you apply, you'll be contacted within 48 hours. You'll need to confirm your place and submit required documentation within the timeframe we provide — places don't hold indefinitely.

What to Have Ready

Your NDIS participant number, support coordinator contact details, and a letter of support from your coordinator. Full document requirements are explained below.

Disability & Access Requirements

This information helps us prepare the right environment and support. All information is strictly confidential and used solely for program delivery.

Program Selection

Eligibility: Sound Haven programs are open to NDIS participants with a physical disability or a neurodivergent condition (e.g. ASD, ADHD, ABI, psychosocial disability). All ages welcome.

How intake works: Sound Haven runs fixed intake rounds throughout the year. After submitting this application, we'll confirm availability for the upcoming intake and contact you with your options. You'll need to respond and complete documentation within the timeframe provided to secure your place.

Required Documents

The following documents are required before a place can be confirmed. Upload here or email to soundhavenau@gmail.com after submitting your application.

📋
Support Coordinator Letter of SupportREQUIRED

A signed letter from your NDIS support coordinator confirming you are fit and able to attend Sound Haven programs. The letter should include the coordinator's name, organisation, contact details, your NDIS participant number, and confirmation of relevant Capacity Building funding.

📄
NDIS Plan Funding PageREQUIRED

A copy of the relevant section of your NDIS plan showing Capacity Building funding under Social & Community Participation or Improved Daily Living, with sufficient funds for the program.

💉
Medical Clearance (if applicable)

If you have complex medical needs, a brief note from a GP or specialist may be requested. We'll advise you during the consultation call.
