Our Programs

Two programs. Crafted to take you somewhere.

DJ & Mixing or Music Production — both run in-person at our Melbourne studio. Fully equipped, fully accessible, and built around you and your NDIS goals. No experience required.

Program 01
DJ & Mixing — In Person

DJ & Mixing

10-WEEK PROGRAM · MELBOURNE STUDIO

Hands-on DJing and live mixing in our fully equipped studio. From the first time you touch the decks to performing your own set — this program takes you all the way there, at your own pace.

Tactile, immediate, and deeply satisfying — DJing is one of the most effective formats we run for building focus, sensory regulation, and genuine confidence in people who haven't responded to traditional supports.

Sensory Regulation Focus & Attention Social Confidence Independence Routine Building
Duration10 Weeks — one program block
Sessions2 × 90 minutes per week
LocationMelbourne, VIC (fully accessible)
EquipmentAll provided on-site
ExperienceNone required — ever
EligibilityNDIS participants — physical or neurodivergent
What You'll Learn

From zero experience to your own live set.

01

Controller Basics

Get comfortable with the gear — cueing, playing, navigating. Zero experience assumed.

02

Beatmatching & Mixing

Tempo, phrasing, transitions. The craft of moving seamlessly between tracks.

03

EQ, FX & Sound

Shape and colour your sound in real time with filters, effects, and EQ.

04

Set Building & Performance

Curation, energy, structure. Build a real set and perform it.

Why This Program

DJing clicks for people who haven't clicked with anything else.

The feedback loop between action and sound is almost instant — which makes it uniquely effective for building focus, sensory regulation, and emotional regulation in people who haven't connected with traditional supports. Neurodivergent participants in particular often find it clicks in a way nothing else has.

It also builds community. The studio becomes a shared space where music is a common language and isolation starts to shrink.

Where These Skills Go in the Real World

Perform at events, community gatherings, and local venues
Work as a DJ at parties, weddings, or corporate events
Build a social media presence and creative content brand
Explore careers in the live music and events industry
Use music as a daily tool for emotional regulation and wellbeing
Program 02
Music Production — In Person

Music Production

10-WEEK BLOCKS · MELBOURNE STUDIO

Beat making, sound design, arrangement, and music production using Ableton Live and FL Studio. Start with one 10-week block and see where it takes you. Come back for a second block and go deeper — more technical, more advanced, more you.

Everything is provided on-site. No prior music knowledge needed. Just the curiosity to make something.

Block 1 — Foundations

Beat making, sampling, sound design basics, DAW navigation, and building your first arrangements.

Block 2 — Advanced Production

Deeper arrangement, mixing, mastering, AI tools, and completing a full production-ready track.

Executive Function Emotional Regulation Vocational Pathway Creative Expression
Block Structure10 weeks per block
Sessions2 × 90 minutes per week
LocationMelbourne, VIC (fully accessible)
SoftwareAbleton Live & FL Studio (on-site)
EligibilityNDIS — physical or neurodivergent
What You'll Learn

From your first beat to something you're genuinely proud of.

01

Beat Making

Rhythm programming, drum patterns, groove, and swing.

02

Sound Design

Synthesis, sampling, and shaping sounds from scratch.

03

Arrangement

Song structure, progression, and building a full track.

04

Mix & Master

Polishing your track so it's ready to share with the world.

Why This Program

Production teaches you to finish things — and that changes how you see yourself.

The process of building a track from scratch — planning, making decisions, handling frustration, feeling the satisfaction of finishing — is an exercise in executive function, emotional regulation, and sustained focus that can't be replicated in a classroom.

Block 1 gives participants a strong, structured foundation. If they choose to return for Block 2, they go further — more technical depth, more creative complexity, and a finished body of work they're genuinely proud of.

Where These Skills Go in the Real World

Release original music on Spotify, Apple Music, and all major platforms
Produce beats for other artists and earn from your work
Score and produce music for film, video, and online content
Pursue a career as a music producer, sound designer, or audio engineer
Start a freelance production business on your own terms
NDIS Funding

Built to link to your plan. Clear to your coordinator from day one.

Both programs align clearly with NDIS Capacity Building funding categories. We work directly with support coordinators to document outcomes, track progress, and ensure every session earns its place in the participant's plan.

Open to NDIS participants with physical disability or neurodivergent conditions.
