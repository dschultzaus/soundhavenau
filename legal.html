Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Sound Haven ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, date of birth, email address, and phone number submitted via our contact or application forms
  • NDIS participant number, plan details, and support coordinator contact information
  • Disability, medical, and access requirement information shared for the purpose of program enrolment
  • Communication history, including emails and enquiries
  • General website usage data via analytics tools (non-identifying)

We collect sensitive health and disability information only where voluntarily provided in the context of enrolment or service delivery, and treat it with the highest level of confidentiality.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and facilitate program enrolment
  • Coordinate with NDIS support coordinators and plan managers
  • Deliver, personalise, and improve our programs and services
  • Comply with our obligations as an NDIS provider
  • Send relevant communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • Maintain records as required by law or NDIS practice standards

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • NDIS support coordinators, plan managers, or other providers — only with your explicit consent
  • Service providers assisting in our operations, bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Regulatory bodies, the NDIA, or law enforcement where required by law

Your Rights & Data Retention

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at soundhavenau@gmail.com. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes collected or as required by law.

Our Services

Sound Haven provides music-based capacity building programs for NDIS participants. Our programs are not a substitute for medical, therapeutic, or clinical services. We are not registered music therapists.

  • Program availability is subject to intake rounds and may change without notice
  • Pricing is confirmed at the time of enrolment
  • NDIS funding suitability must be confirmed with your support coordinator prior to enrolment
  • All participants must hold an active NDIS plan with appropriate Capacity Building funding at the time of enrolment
  • A place is not confirmed until required documentation has been verified and a consultation call completed
