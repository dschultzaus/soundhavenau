Who We Are

A place to create, connect, and build something worth showing up for.

Sound Haven is a creative studio in Melbourne, built for people with physical disability and neurodivergent individuals who are ready to engage, create, and grow through music.

We don't just run programs. We build pathways — linking every session to the goals in your NDIS plan and the life you actually want to be living.
